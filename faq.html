Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about booking your dream vacation

01 Booking & Planning

Why do I need a travel agent?
A travel agent will handle all your bookings and be your point of contact with the supplier, taking all the stress out of planning, organizing, and finalizing the vacation. B Free Travel will handle all support issues that may arise and provide expert recommendations.
Can you book groups?
Almost every supplier has group rates, which can reduce the overall price of a trip. Whether it is a corporate outing, a family vacation, or a friends trip, group bookings are available!
Do you specialize in only one type of vacation?
B Free Travel utilizes many suppliers, from all inclusive resorts to Disney vacations to cruises, even hotel bookings. If you can think of a place to travel to, B Free will work to make it the best vacation ever!
I have a general type of vacation or location in mind, is that enough to get started?
Of course! A big part of a travel agent's job is to research and provide as much information on vacation destinations as possible, so you have everything you need to make the final decision on where you want to go. You should feel secure knowing that B Free Travel has the knowledge and tools to build your vacation from the ground up.
How far in advance should I book my trip?
The general guidelines for booking vacations is:
  • Cruises: 6-18 months
  • International Travel: 6-12 months
  • Disney: 6-18 months
  • Domestic Travel: 3-6 months
Help! I need to book a vacation or hotel last minute. Can you do it?
I will do my best to work with suppliers to find you a last minute vacation, depending on availability.

02 Pricing & Payments

How much does it cost to use a travel agent?
There is no extra cost for you to use a travel agent. The agent is paid by the supplier. The cost of your vacation is the same whether you book with an agent or not and the commission is only paid after the trip is complete. Agents are usually able to find better prices using their suppliers over an individual booking.
Can I use a payment plan?
Many suppliers offer payment plans. When using B Free Travel, you do not pay me, rather you pay the supplier directly, which allows you to utilize their payment plans.
Are there any hidden fees?
No. At B Free Travel, all prices are upfront and transparent. B Free will never charge you more than the supplier and the prices will be communicated clearly during the booking process.

03 Travel Documents

Do I need a passport or visa?
Depending on the location of your travel, a passport is always recommended and is necessary for all international travel. US passports take an average of 4-6 weeks for standard processing and may be expedited for a fee to receive them earlier. Certain countries require visas for travel and this information will be provided to you if your vacation is set for a country that needs one.
Can you help me with my travel documentation?
While you are responsible for obtaining and maintaining your own documents, B Free Travel will guide you through filling out entry paperwork for countries and verify that you have all the necessary documents needed to travel.

04 Changes & Cancellations

What happens if I need to cancel my trip?
Sometimes life happens and trips need to be cancelled. Many suppliers offer a grace period for trip cancellation, with only a deposit being forfeited. It is always recommended to invest in travel insurance.
Can I make changes after booking a vacation?
Most suppliers will allow changes within reason. Altering dates may alter the overall price, but with enough notice and availability, changes usually can be made.
Should I look into travel insurance?
Travel insurance is highly recommended. For the price that a vacation costs, you don't want unexpected events to cause a forfeiture of your money. Many suppliers carry their own insurance, which will give a refund in either credits or repayment, and it usually carries a small fee that is added onto the overall total. Dependent on the supplier and the travel insurance company, they can cover most cancellations from medical issues to the loss of a job and sometimes even for no designated reason. The security that travel insurance gives to you and your family is indescribable and should not be overlooked.

What if my flight is cancelled or delayed?
